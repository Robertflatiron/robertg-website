Welcome To My Build!

Hello all, my name is Robert G and here I will discuss a few events which
took place while owning my motorcycle. I will also go over some
of the modifications I have made to compare to stock.
I hope you all enjoy!

Stock (no modifications)

  • The exhaust system was OEM (from the factory)
  • The motorcycle was not "lowered" for better aerodynamics
  • Motorcycle has the OEM air filter

Modified

  • I purchased an exhaust system
  • I purchased a Sprint Air Filter
  • I purchased a QuickShifter
  • I "lowered" the motorcycle for better aerodynamics
  • I have flashed and tuned the ECU
Upon completion of these modifications, my motorcycle was stolen from me. But with the assistance of the GPS which was installed on the motorcycle, I was able to retrieve it.
I assisted the Dallas Police Department in the retrieval of my motorcycle, along with the physical arrest of the criminal.
As a foot chase commence, the DPD officer gave me the verbal comand to "get him". He did this because he was not fast enough to catch the criminal.

Thank you all for visiting my page and I hope you were able to get a little feeling into what I went through to.
